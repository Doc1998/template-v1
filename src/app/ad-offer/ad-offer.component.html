WEB DESIGN IRELAND : OUR PROCESS

DISCOVERY

Before we start coding your new website there is some steps we need to make to ensure we produce the best product. We need to perform an indepth look into your business & discuss your business needs and goals so we can set a plan to meet your high level goals and expectations. To jumpstart this process we will ask you to fill in a questionarre and possibly jump on a call with us to ensure we are the same page. You can chose to work with us to write the copy for your website or you can leave that to our professional copywriter to write engaging content that will get picked up by search engines.

STRATEGY

Once we have completed the discovery stage and have set clear expectations for the goal of the web design, we will produce a sitemap with the structure of the web pages. During this stage we will identify problems & solutions and also offer our best recommendations

WEB DESIGN & WEB DEVELOPMENT

This is the stage where we hand code your web design solution. We will provide you with a website that satisfies Google's core metrics and provides customers with a smooth experience designed to convert into sales for your business

LAUNCH

Once design and development is complete we will provide your with a test domain to ensure you are happy with the look and feel of the website and give you an opportunity to request edits and changes. Once you confirm you are ready to launch, we will host your website & set up your new website with Analytics tracking. Post launch we will provide unlimited edits and support so your website is always up to date.
